About the Artist

Born from a turbulent childhood marked by family conflict, he transforms pain into vibrant canvases that speak of resilience and quiet hope. He carries forward his father’s artistic legacy, creating pieces that feel both intimate and universal.

An IIT Roorkee B.Tech and JNU M.Tech graduate who codes by day at Capgemini, he blends technical precision with a wide curiosity—astrology, politics and economics inform his vision. Married to Pooja and father of a 17-year-old daughter, Hita. His paintings invite collectors to connect with stories of survival, wisdom and understated beauty.
