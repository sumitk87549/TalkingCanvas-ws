Shipping and Delivery Policy

Shipping Methods

We offer multiple shipping options to meet your needs. All paintings are carefully packaged to ensure safe delivery.

Delivery Times

Standard delivery typically takes 5-7 business days. Express delivery options are available for an additional fee.

Tracking

Once your order ships, you will receive a tracking number via email to monitor your delivery status.

International Shipping

We ship internationally to most countries. International orders may take 2-4 weeks for delivery.
