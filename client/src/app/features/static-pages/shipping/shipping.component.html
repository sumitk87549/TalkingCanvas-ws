Shipping & Delivery Policy

Last Updated: November 24, 2024

We take great care in packaging and shipping your artwork to ensure it arrives safely. Please review our shipping policy for information about delivery times, costs, and procedures.

1. Shipping Methods

We offer multiple shipping options to meet your needs and budget. All paintings are carefully packaged with professional-grade materials to ensure safe delivery.

1.1 Domestic Shipping (India)

Standard Shipping

Delivery Time: 5-7 business days

Cost: ₹500 - ₹1,500 (based on size and weight)

Reliable delivery through trusted courier partners with basic tracking.

Express Shipping

Delivery Time: 2-3 business days

Cost: ₹1,500 - ₹3,000 (based on size and weight)

Priority handling with expedited delivery and comprehensive tracking.

Premium Shipping

Delivery Time: 1-2 business days

Cost: ₹3,000 - ₹5,000 (based on size and weight)

White-glove service with insurance, real-time tracking, and signature required.

We ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping times and costs vary based on destination, customs procedures, and shipping method selected.

International Standard

Economical international shipping with tracking.

International Express

Delivery Time: 5-10 business days

Expedited international delivery with comprehensive insurance and tracking.

2. Packaging

Your artwork's safety is our top priority. We use professional-grade packaging materials specifically designed for fine art:

  • Protective Wrapping: Acid-free glassine paper to protect the painting surface
  • Corner Protection: Custom-cut foam corners to prevent damage
  • Rigid Support: Cardboard backing or wooden crates for larger pieces
  • Moisture Protection: Sealed plastic wrapping to protect against humidity
  • Outer Box: Double-walled corrugated boxes with "Fragile" and "This Side Up" markings
  • Insurance: All shipments are insured for their full value

3. Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-3 business days. Processing time includes:

  • Order verification and payment confirmation
  • Professional packaging of your artwork
  • Quality inspection before shipping
  • Generation of shipping labels and documentation

Note: Custom or commissioned pieces may require additional processing time, which will be communicated at the time of order.

4. Tracking Your Order

Once your order ships, you will receive:

  • Email notification with tracking number
  • Direct link to track your shipment
  • Estimated delivery date
  • Carrier contact information

You can also track your order anytime by logging into your account and viewing your order history.

5. Delivery

5.1 Delivery Requirements

For your protection and to ensure secure delivery:

  • Signature may be required upon delivery
  • Someone must be available to receive the package
  • Delivery to PO Boxes is not available for paintings
  • Accurate delivery address must be provided at checkout

5.2 Delivery Attempts

If delivery cannot be completed:

  • Carrier will make up to 3 delivery attempts
  • You will receive notification of each attempt
  • Package may be held at local facility for pickup
  • After final attempt, package will be returned to us

6. International Shipping Details

6.1 Customs and Duties

For international orders:

  • Customs duties and taxes are the responsibility of the buyer
  • Fees vary by country and are collected upon delivery
  • We declare accurate value on customs forms
  • Delays may occur during customs clearance

6.2 Restricted Countries

We currently do not ship to countries under international trade sanctions or with unreliable postal services. Please contact us if you have questions about shipping to your location.

7. Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on:

  • Painting dimensions and weight
  • Destination address
  • Selected shipping method
  • Insurance value

Free Shipping: We offer free standard shipping on orders over ₹50,000 within India.

8. Damaged or Lost Shipments

8.1 Inspection Upon Delivery

Please inspect your package immediately upon delivery:

  • Check for visible damage to the outer packaging
  • Note any damage on the delivery receipt
  • Take photos if damage is evident
  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery

8.2 Claims Process

If your painting arrives damaged or is lost in transit:

  • Contact us immediately with photos and tracking information
  • We will file a claim with the carrier
  • We will arrange for replacement or full refund
  • All shipments are fully insured

9. Address Changes

If you need to change your shipping address:

  • Contact us immediately if order has not yet shipped
  • Once shipped, address changes may not be possible
  • Additional fees may apply for address changes
  • Ensure accuracy when placing your order
