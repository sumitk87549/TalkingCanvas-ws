Return and Refund Policy

Return Period

Items can be returned within 30 days of purchase if they are in original condition and packaging.

Return Process

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team with your order number and reason for return.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your returned item, we will process your refund within 7-10 business days.

Shipping Costs

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to a defect or error on our part.
