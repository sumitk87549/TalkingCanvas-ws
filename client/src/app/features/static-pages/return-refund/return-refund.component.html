Return & Refund Policy

Last Updated: November 25, 2025

Your satisfaction is our top priority. We want you to be completely happy with your artwork purchase. Please review our comprehensive return and refund policy below for information about returns, exchanges, and refunds.

1. Return Period

We offer a 30-day return window from the date of purchase for all original artwork. Returns are accepted provided the artwork is returned in the same condition and packaging as received.

1.1 Eligible Items

  • All original artwork and paintings
  • Limited edition prints (with certificate of authenticity)
  • Custom commissioned pieces (subject to approval)
  • Acrylic, oil, and watercolor paintings

1.2 Non-Eligible Items

  • Sold out or custom-ordered items after 14 days
  • Items damaged due to misuse or accidents
  • Items with removed hang tags or authenticity certificates
  • Vinyl decals or temporary artworks

2. Return Process

2.1 Initiating a Return

To start your return process:

  1. Contact our customer service team at pkumar.mail@gmail.com
  2. Provide your order number and reason for return
  3. Receive return authorization and shipping instructions
  4. Package your artwork securely for transport

2.2 Packaging Requirements

When preparing your artwork for return:

  • Use the original packaging if available
  • Wrap the artwork in protective materials
  • Include all accessories, certificates, and documentation
  • Take photos of the item before shipping

3. Return Shipping

3.1 Shipping Costs

Return shipping costs depend on several factors:

  • Customer Responsibility: Standard returns not due to our error
  • Seller Pays: Returns due to defect, damage, or wrong item sent
  • Prepaid Labels: Provided for approved returns at our expense
  • Insurance Coverage: We recommend insuring all return shipments

3.2 Shipping Methods

Economy Return

Time: 7-14 business days

Cost: ₹300 - ₹800 (customer pays)

Cost-effective return shipping with basic tracking.

Express Return

Time: 3-5 business days

Cost: ₹800 - ₹1,500 (customer pays)

Priority return processing and expedited delivery.

4. Inspection & Processing

4.1 Quality Inspection

Once we receive your return, our team conducts a thorough inspection:

  • Verification of artwork condition and originality
  • Documentation of any damage or wear
  • Validation of return eligibility
  • Photography for quality control records

4.2 Processing Time

Return processing typically takes 5-7 business days after receipt:

  • Initial inspection and verification
  • Refund approval and processing
  • Restocking of eligible items
  • Notification of final resolution

5. Refunds

5.1 Refund Types

  • Full Refund: Purchase price plus applicable taxes
  • Partial Refund: In cases of condition issues
  • Store Credit: Available upon request
  • Exchange: For different items of equal or lesser value

5.2 Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed within 3-5 business days of return approval:

  • Credit Cards: 5-10 business days to appear on statement
  • PayPal: Immediate refund to PayPal account
  • Bank Transfer: 3-5 business days to account
  • Store Credit: Instant credit to account for future purchases

Note: Original shipping charges are not refunded unless the return is due to our error.

6. Special Circumstances

6.1 Damaged Items

If your artwork arrives damaged during shipping:

  • Contact us within 48 hours with photos
  • Full refund or replacement at our expense
  • Return shipping labels provided
  • Process completed within 10 business days

6.2 Wrong Item Sent

If you receive the wrong artwork:

  • Notify us immediately upon receipt
  • Return shipping at our expense
  • Correct item shipped immediately
  • Full refund if return is preferred

6.3 Custom Orders

Custom commissioned artwork returns:

  • Subject to case-by-case approval
  • May require design modification discussion
  • Return shipping costs determine eligibility
  • Final decision based on production status

7. International Returns

7.1 Customs and Duties

For international orders:

  • Customs duties and taxes are non-refundable
  • Return shipping costs vary by destination
  • Local customs regulations may apply
  • Professional customs brokerage recommended

7.2 Restricted Countries

We do not accept returns from countries under international trade sanctions. Please contact our team for assistance with your specific location.

8. Exchanges

We offer exchanges for different items of equal or lesser value within 30 days. Exchange possibilities include:

  • Different size or style of same artwork
  • Alternative pieces within our current collection
  • Custom exchange requests considered
  • Additional payment required for higher value items

Important: Exchanges require prior approval and may incur additional shipping costs.

Our customer service team is available to assist you throughout the return process.
