Hello! Let's Keep This Simple.

We believe privacy policies should be easy to understand, not filled with legal jargon. Here's exactly what we do with your information - in plain English.

What Information We Collect (And Why)

When You Browse

What: Pages you visit, paintings you look at
Why: To show you more of what you like
Stored: Anonymous data only - we don't know it's "you"

When You Buy

What: Name, email, shipping address, payment info
Why: To deliver your painting and send you updates
Payment: We use secure payment processors (Stripe/PayPal). We never see or store your full card details - only they do.

When You Create an Account (Optional)

What: Email and password
Why: So you can track orders and save favorites
Your Choice: You don't need an account to buy

What We DON'T Do

  • We don't sell your data. Ever. To anyone.
  • We don't spam you. You'll only get order updates and (if you choose) occasional emails about new paintings.
  • We don't track you across the internet. What you do on other websites is your business.
  • We don't share your info with advertisers. We're not interested in that business model.

What We DO With Your Information

  • Ship your painting - We need your address for that!
  • Send order updates - "Your painting has shipped!" type emails
  • Provide customer support - If you have questions or issues
  • Improve our website - Understanding what paintings people love helps us grow
  • Legal requirements only - If required by law (very rare for a small art business)

Your Choices & Rights

You Control Your Data

Want to know what we have? Email us - we'll send it within 7 days.

Want us to delete everything? Just ask - we'll erase all your personal data (except order history required by tax law for 7 years).

Want to stop emails? Click "unsubscribe" in any email. You'll still get order updates, but nothing else.

Want to change your info? Log into your account or email us.

Made a mistake? Contact us within 24 hours of ordering and we'll help you fix it.

Cookies (The Digital Kind)

We use a few cookies to make the website work:

  • Essential cookies: Remember your cart items
  • Preference cookies: Remember your language choice
  • Analytics cookies: Help us see which paintings are popular (anonymous data)

No account needed: You can decline optional cookies and still buy paintings - your cart will just reset when you close the browser.

International Shipping & Data

We ship to: UK, Australia, Norway, Denmark, France, Italy, and more European countries.

Your data may be processed in: India (where we're based) and the country you're in.

Your rights: You have strong privacy rights under GDPR (Europe), UK Data Protection Act, and other local laws. We respect them all.

Children's Privacy

Our paintings are for adults to buy. We don't knowingly collect information from anyone under 16.

Security

  • Encrypted connections: Your data travels securely (SSL/TLS encryption)
  • Secure servers: Your information is stored safely
  • Limited access: Only the business owner and essential staff can see customer data
  • No data breaches yet: And we work hard to keep it that way

If something goes wrong: We'll email you immediately and explain what happened and what we're doing about it.

Changes to This Policy

If we change anything important, we'll:

  1. Update the date at the top
  2. Email all customers with the changes
  3. Keep the old version available for 90 days

Small updates (like fixing typos) won't trigger emails.

Questions? Concerns?

This is a small, family-run business. There's a real person reading your emails.

Contact us:

Email: your-email@domain.com

Response time: Usually within 24 hours (48 hours on weekends)

Complaints: If you're in Europe/UK and unhappy with how we handle your data, you can also contact your local data protection authority. We'd prefer to resolve it directly with you first!

The Bottom Line

We're artists and small business owners, not a big corporation. We collect only what we need to send you beautiful paintings. We don't play games with your data. We treat your information the way we'd want ours treated.

That's it. Really.
