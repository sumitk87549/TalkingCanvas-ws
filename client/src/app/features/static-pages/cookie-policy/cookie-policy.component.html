Cookie Policy

Last Updated: November 25, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Talking Canvas uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience. We are committed to being transparent about our cookie practices and respecting your privacy choices.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They help us:

  • Store your preferences and settings
  • Remember your login status and account information
  • Analyze website traffic and usage patterns
  • Personalize your browsing experience
  • Enable third-party services and features

Cookies may also be used by our third-party service providers to deliver services on our behalf.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly:

  • Authentication Cookies: Keep you logged in during your session
  • Session Cookies: Remember your shopping cart and form submissions
  • Security Cookies: Enable security features and prevent fraud
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your language and region preferences

2.2 Analytics and Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website:

  • Google Analytics for website usage analysis
  • Traffic source and conversion tracking
  • Page view and session duration metrics
  • Error and crash reporting data

2.3 Marketing and Advertising Cookies

These cookies are used to deliver relevant advertisements:

  • Remarketing and retargeting services
  • Social media pixel tracking
  • Affiliate marketing program cookies
  • Personalized content recommendations

2.4 Third-Party Cookies

We also use third-party services that set their own cookies:

  • Social media sharing plugins (Facebook, Instagram, Pinterest)
  • Payment processing services
  • Customer support chat widgets
  • CDN and content delivery services

3. How We Use Cookies

3.1 Improving User Experience

Cookies help us create a better browsing experience:

  • Remembering your login status and preferences
  • Maintaining your shopping cart between sessions
  • Keeping track of items you've viewed recently
  • Displaying relevant content based on your interests

3.2 Analytics and Insights

We use cookies to gather analytical data:

  • Website traffic patterns and user behavior
  • Popular pages and art categories
  • Conversion funnels and user journeys
  • Error tracking and performance monitoring

3.3 Marketing and Personalization

Cookies enable personalized marketing:

  • Showing relevant artwork recommendations
  • Displaying personalized promotions and offers
  • Retargeting campaigns based on browsing behavior
  • Social media advertising optimization

4. Cookie Consent and Management

4.1 Cookie Banner

When you first visit our website, you will see a cookie consent banner that allows you to:

  • Accept all cookies
  • Reject non-essential cookies
  • Customize your cookie preferences
  • Learn more about each cookie category

4.2 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings:

  • Block or delete all cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Allow cookies from certain websites
  • Clear cookies when you close your browser

Note: Disabling cookies may limit the functionality of our website and affect your user experience.

4.3 Cookie Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time by:

  • Visiting our cookie settings page
  • Clicking the cookie settings link in our footer
  • Contacting our privacy team directly
  • Using your browser's privacy settings

5. Cookie Retention Periods

We use different retention periods based on cookie type:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Typically last from 30 days to 2 years
  • Google Analytics: 2 years for traffic analysis
  • Marketing Cookies: Usually 90 days to 1 year
  • Authentication Cookies: 30 days for security reasons

We regularly review and update our cookie retention periods to comply with privacy regulations.
