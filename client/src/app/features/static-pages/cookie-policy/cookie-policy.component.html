Cookie Policy

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and improve your experience on our website.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to remember your login information, track your browsing behavior, and personalize your experience.

Types of Cookies

We use both session cookies (temporary) and persistent cookies (long-term) to enhance your browsing experience.

Managing Cookies

You can control cookie settings in your browser preferences. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.
