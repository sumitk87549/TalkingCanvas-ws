Meet the Artist

Praveen Kumar

Engineer by Day, Artist by Heart

Born from a turbulent childhood marked by family conflict, Praveen transforms pain into vibrant canvases that speak of resilience and quiet hope. He carries forward his father's artistic legacy, creating pieces that feel both intimate and universal.

An IIT Roorkee B.Tech and JNU M.Tech graduate who codes by day at Capgemini, Praveen blends technical precision with wide-ranging curiosity. His interests in astrology, politics, and economics inform his artistic vision, creating works that are intellectually engaging and emotionally resonant.

Married to Pooja and father to 17-year-old Hita, Praveen's paintings invite collectors to connect with stories of survival, wisdom, and understated beauty. Each piece is a testament to the human spirit's capacity to create beauty from adversity.
